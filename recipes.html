Cake!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups flour

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, cream together the sugar and butter.
  3. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  4. Stir in the flour until well blended.
  5. Pour batter into a greased 9x9 inch pan.
  6. Bake for 30 to 40 minutes in the preheated oven.
  7. Cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack.
  8. Let cool completely before serving.
  9. Enjoy your cake!
