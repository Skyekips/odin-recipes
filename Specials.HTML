Redbull Special

This will be how to make a redbull special!

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 can of Redbull
  • 1/4 cup of flavor of choice
  • half cup cream
  • 14 or so icecubes

Instructions

  1. Pour the redbull into a glass
  2. Add the flavor of choice
  3. Add the cream
  4. Add the icecubes
  5. Stir or blend in blender and enjoy!
